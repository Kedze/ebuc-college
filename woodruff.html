Chief Apostle Dr Jerry Woodruff

Nothing is more influential in shaping and molding our world than a yielded vessel to carry the message of Christ’s hope and deliverance for all. These attributes are found in Overseer/Apostle Dr: Jerry L. Woodruff. He has been chosen to usher in a dynamic and powerful move of God. Like Nehemiah, he is not afraid of the assignment God has put in his hands to accomplish. His burning vision to see the lives of all mankind transformed by the revelation of God’s word is evident in his commitment to ministry and his lifestyle of holiness.
The Man Apostle Woodruff,
is a native of Stockton California and was reared under the tutelage of his grandmother – Pastor Leta McPherson in the Stockton Tabernacle Pentecostal Church Of God Worldwide.
He accepted the Lord Jesus Christ as his savior and the call to ministry at a young age 7 years old in 1966. He also was under the tutelage of Bishop German Ross. who were all Founders and Leaders of the Church of God In Christ? During his tenure in the ministry, Apostle Woodruff held numerous positions of leadership including .In L C Patten of the north west states, he was elevated as one of the Elders of Church Of God In Christ under the jurisdictional prelate, Bishop G,E, Paterson. The Ministry In Living Waters international fellowship, Apostle acknowledged God’s inspiration to move forward into full – time evangelistic ministry.
By faith he became a “spiritual nomad” following each ordered step worldwide teaching and preaching. His mantle is prophetic in purpose, apostolic in function, pastoral in compassion, and evangelistic in outreach. Founder Living Waters Eastern Bible University College
livingwatersebuc.com

The Movement years after traveling as a full time Evangelist, God compelled him to step into a new dimension of service to the office of a Bishop. In the church of god in Christ, he obeyed the voice of God and established Living Waters Eastern National Fellowship. His objective is to become a spiritual entry point for God and the Angelic hosts to reach the World. After the birth of Living Waters, God favored and blessed Apostle Woodruff to establish many Ministries, Programs and Workshops that would minister to all ethnic backgrounds on a weekly basis – Feeding Body, Soul and Spirit. It is Apostle Woodruff’s prayer that the preaching uttered from his lips be both an outreach and an in-reach to all that hear the Word and that the spoken Word be filled with compassion, revelation and a true deliverance experience that changes the lives of the masses. He enjoys studying the Word of God, Educating those on Finances, Family, Travel and Music.
